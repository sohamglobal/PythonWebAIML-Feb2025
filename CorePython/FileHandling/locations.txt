March is a great time to visit India, as the weather is generally pleasant across most regions. Here are some top tourist destinations to consider:

1. Rajasthan (Jaipur, Udaipur, Jaisalmer, Jodhpur)
Why Visit? Pleasant weather, historical forts, palaces, and vibrant culture.
Top Attractions: Amber Fort (Jaipur), City Palace (Udaipur), Jaisalmer Fort, Mehrangarh Fort (Jodhpur).
Transportation: Well-connected by air (Jaipur, Udaipur), rail, and road. Local transport includes taxis, auto-rickshaws, and buses.
2. Kerala (Munnar, Alleppey, Kochi)
Why Visit? Lush green landscapes, backwaters, and tea plantations.
Top Attractions: Munnar Tea Gardens, Alleppey Houseboats, Fort Kochi.
Transportation: Kochi has an international airport. Trains and buses connect major cities. Houseboats and ferries operate in backwaters.
3. Goa
Why Visit? Warm weather, beautiful beaches, and lively nightlife.
Top Attractions: Baga Beach, Dudhsagar Waterfalls, Basilica of Bom Jesus.
Transportation: Dabolim Airport (Goa) has flights from major cities. Trains and buses are also available.
4. Himachal Pradesh (Shimla, Manali, Dharamshala)
Why Visit? Cool climate, scenic landscapes, adventure sports.
Top Attractions: Rohtang Pass, Mall Road (Shimla), Dalai Lama Temple (Dharamshala).
Transportation: Nearest airports are in Chandigarh and Kullu. Trains reach Kalka, and buses/taxis connect to hill stations.
5. Varanasi (Uttar Pradesh)
Why Visit? Spiritual experiences, Ganga Aarti, historic temples.
Top Attractions: Kashi Vishwanath Temple, Dashashwamedh Ghat.
Transportation: Lal Bahadur Shastri Airport (Varanasi) has domestic flights. Well-connected by trains and road.
6. Andaman & Nicobar Islands
Why Visit? Tropical beaches, coral reefs, and water activities.
Top Attractions: Radhanagar Beach, Cellular Jail, Scuba Diving.
Transportation: Flights from major cities to Port Blair. Ferries connect islands.
